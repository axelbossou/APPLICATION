I am the Tunisian citizen Ahmed Hanoun, born in the fifties in the city of Tozeur, Bilad El-Jerid, which was known for its rich oases and the attraction of desert tourists in the southwest. Since my youth I have loved this field... I worked as a tour guide for years and moved to all our tourist resorts to accompany tourists and provide them with the necessary information during trips.
